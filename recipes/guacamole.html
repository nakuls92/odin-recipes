Guacamole

Description

Fresh guacamole recipe that serves 4

Ingredients

  • 3 Avacados
  • 1 Lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced Onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avacados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
